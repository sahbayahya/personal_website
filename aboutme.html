ABOUT

Brief Information

Sanghyuk Chun is now M.S. Candidate at the Department of Electrical Engineering in KAIST (Korea Advanced Institude of Science and Technology), Korea. His major research interests include machine learning, data mining and system design. Currently he researches on developing and analyzing algorithm for solving subspace clustering optimization problem. He graduated KAIST with B.S. degree in the electrical engineering and management science department in February 2014, and worked as web server and front developer at web service company named IUM-SOCIUS in June 2012 to January 2013.

Research Interest

My research interests focus on the machine learning.

My major interest, machine Learning is about the construction and study of systems that can learn from data. (wikipedia) Basically, my researches more focus on real massive data analysis or prediction based on mathmetical methodolgy such as robust PCA, subspace clustering, belief propagation and deep learning. Recently I am interested in developing fast and efficient algorithm for robust subspace clustering problem.
